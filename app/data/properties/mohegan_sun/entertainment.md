# Mohegan Sun — Entertainment

Mohegan Sun is one of the premier entertainment destinations in the northeastern United States, hosting hundreds of events each year across multiple world-class venues.

## Venues

### Mohegan Sun Arena
- **Capacity**: 10,000 seats
- **Location**: Casino of the Sky
- **Description**: A state-of-the-art arena that hosts major concerts, championship boxing, UFC events, comedy specials, family shows, and more. The arena has welcomed legendary performers including Elton John, Billy Joel, Beyoncé, Drake, and many more. The arena features excellent sightlines from every seat, premium luxury suites, and floor seating options. Named one of the top venues of its size in the world.
- **Box Office Hours**: Monday–Saturday 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM, Sunday 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM (extended hours on event days)
- **Tickets**: Available at the box office, online at mohegansun.com, or through Ticketmaster

### Wolf Den
- **Capacity**: 350 seats
- **Location**: Casino of the Sky
- **Description**: A unique, intimate performance venue offering **free live entertainment** almost every night of the week. The Wolf Den features a mix of tribute bands, classic rock acts, R&B performers, comedians, and emerging artists. No tickets required — seating is first-come, first-served. This is a beloved guest favorite and one of the best-kept entertainment values in the region. Shows typically start at 8:00 PM on weekdays and 8:00 PM and 10:00 PM on weekends.
- **Cost**: Free — no cover charge, no tickets needed

### The Cabaret Theatre
- **Capacity**: 300 seats
- **Location**: Casino of the Sky
- **Description**: An intimate cabaret-style venue hosting comedy shows, tribute acts, spoken-word performances, and special engagements. Features cocktail-table seating and a full bar. Check the events calendar for the current lineup and ticket prices.

### Comix Roadhouse
- **Capacity**: 700 seats
- **Location**: Casino of the Earth
- **Hours**: Show times vary; doors typically open 1 hour before showtime
- **Description**: A world-class comedy venue that has hosted the biggest names in stand-up comedy, including Dave Chappelle, Kevin Hart, Amy Schumer, and Bill Burr. The venue features a full dinner-and-show experience with a dedicated menu. Shows run Thursday through Sunday. Tickets are available at the box office and online. The 21+ venue provides an energetic, adult-oriented comedy experience.

## Nightlife

### Avalon
- **Location**: Casino of the Sky
- **Hours**: Friday and Saturday 10:00 PM – 2:00 AM (seasonal; check current schedule)
- **Age**: 21+
- **Description**: Mohegan Sun's premier nightclub featuring world-renowned DJs, themed party nights, VIP bottle service, and a state-of-the-art sound and lighting system. The club spans multiple levels with a main dance floor, VIP balcony, and private cabanas. Dress code is enforced: upscale attire required, no sneakers, athletic wear, or hats.

### novelle
- **Location**: Casino of the Sky
- **Hours**: Thursday–Saturday 8:00 PM – 2:00 AM
- **Age**: 21+
- **Description**: An ultra-lounge experience combining craft cocktails, live DJ performances, and a sophisticated atmosphere. Features plush seating, creative lighting design, and an upscale cocktail menu. Perfect for pre- or post-dinner drinks and socializing.

## Seasonal & Special Events

Mohegan Sun hosts numerous special events throughout the year:
- **New Year's Eve Celebration**: Multiple venue parties, live performances, and a midnight countdown
- **Sun Wine & Food Fest**: An annual multi-day wine and food festival featuring celebrity chefs, tastings, and cooking demonstrations (typically held in January/February)
- **PGA Golf Events**: Hosted at the Mohegan Sun Golf Club
- **Championship Boxing & MMA**: Regular fight nights in the arena
- **Holiday Events**: Themed celebrations for major holidays including Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Fourth of July

## Live Music Schedule

Live music is available throughout the resort on most days:
- **Wolf Den**: Free shows nightly (see schedule)
- **Vista Lounge**: Live acoustic sets Thursday–Saturday evenings
- **SolToro**: Live Latin music Friday and Saturday nights
- **The Beer Garden**: Live bands on Saturday afternoons

## Entertainment Tips for Guests

- **Advance Tickets**: For arena shows and Comix Roadhouse, purchasing tickets in advance is recommended as popular shows sell out quickly.
- **Wolf Den Strategy**: For the free Wolf Den shows, arrive at least 30–60 minutes early for popular acts to secure seating.
- **Momentum Members**: May receive early access, discounted tickets, or complimentary tickets to select events based on tier level.
- **Show + Dine Packages**: Some events offer bundled dinner-and-show packages. Check the website or call for current offers.
