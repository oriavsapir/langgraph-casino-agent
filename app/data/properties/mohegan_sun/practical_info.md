# Mohegan Sun — Practical Information

## Getting Around the Resort

Mohegan Sun is a vast property. The two casinos, Casino of the Earth and Casino of the Sky, are connected via the Shops at Mohegan Sun, a covered walkway with moving walkways (people movers) that makes it easy to traverse the resort. Walking from one end to the other takes approximately 10–15 minutes at a leisurely pace.

### Shuttle Service
Complimentary shuttle service is available between the hotel towers and remote parking areas. Shuttles run approximately every 10–15 minutes during peak hours.

## ATM & Banking

- **ATMs**: Located throughout both casinos, the hotel lobbies, and near the parking garages. Standard network fees apply.
- **Cashier Cage**: Full-service cashier cages are located in both Casino of the Earth and Casino of the Sky for check cashing, chip exchange, and jackpot payouts.
- **Credit Services**: Available at the cashier cage for establishing or using a credit line.

## Wi-Fi & Connectivity

- **Wi-Fi**: Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the resort, including hotel rooms, casino floors, restaurants, and public areas.
- **Network Name**: "Mohegan Sun Guest"
- **Business Center**: For guests needing printing, scanning, or computer access, a 24-hour business center is located in the Sky Tower lobby.

## Safety & Security

- Mohegan Sun employs a professional security team that is on-site 24/7
- Security officers are stationed throughout the resort and are available to assist with any concerns
- The resort features comprehensive surveillance systems throughout all public areas
- A first-aid station is available on the casino floor for medical emergencies
- AED (automated external defibrillator) units are located throughout the property

## Lost & Found

If you lose an item during your visit, contact the Security department at (860) 862-8045. Items turned in are held at the Lost & Found office. Claim requests can also be made online through the Mohegan Sun website.

## Coat Check

Complimentary coat check is available near the main entrances of both Casino of the Earth and Casino of the Sky during operating hours. Tips are appreciated but not required.

## Accessibility

Mohegan Sun is fully accessible and compliant with ADA requirements:
- Wheelchair-accessible entrances, elevators, and restrooms throughout the property
- Accessible gaming positions at slots and table games
- Wheelchair rentals available at the information desks
- Service animals are welcome throughout the resort
- TTY phones and hearing-assisted listening devices are available at the arena and event venues
- Accessible hotel rooms available in both towers (see Hotel section)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I bring outside food or beverages into the casino?
Outside food and beverages are not permitted on the casino floor or in restaurants. Sealed, commercially packaged water bottles are generally allowed.

### Is there a dress code for the casino?
The casino floor is casual; there is no strict dress code. However, shoes and shirts are required at all times. Specific restaurants and nightclubs may enforce dress codes (see individual venue listings).

### Can I smoke inside the casino?
Designated smoking sections are available on the casino floor in both casinos. The majority of the gaming floor, all restaurants, hotel rooms, and public areas are non-smoking. A dedicated outdoor smoking area is also available.

### Do you offer gift cards?
Yes, Mohegan Sun gift cards are available at the Momentum desks and select retail locations. Gift cards can be used at participating restaurants, shops, and spa services. They cannot be used for gaming.

### Is there an app?
Yes, the Mohegan Sun mobile app is available for iOS and Android. The app features your Momentum account, property maps, event schedules, restaurant menus, and the ability to book hotel rooms and make dining reservations.

### What if I need to charge my phone?
Phone charging stations are available at several locations on the casino floor. Additionally, portable chargers can be rented from select locations in the Shops at Mohegan Sun.

### Is there nearby accommodation outside the resort?
Several hotels and inns are located within a 15-minute drive of Mohegan Sun in Uncasville, Norwich, and New London. However, staying on-site provides the most convenient access to all resort amenities and events.

### What are the nearby attractions?
- **Mystic Seaport Museum**: 15 minutes away — a living history museum of America's maritime heritage
- **Mystic Aquarium**: 20 minutes away — featuring beluga whales, sea lions, and interactive exhibits
- **Olde Mistick Village**: 15 minutes away — a colonial-style shopping village
- **Submarine Force Museum & USS Nautilus**: 20 minutes away in Groton — free admission
- **Gillette Castle State Park**: 30 minutes away — a medieval-style castle overlooking the Connecticut River

### How do I get to the arena from the hotel?
From the Sky Tower, the arena is directly accessible via the Sky Tower elevators and casino level. From the Earth Tower, follow signs through the Shops at Mohegan Sun to reach Casino of the Sky, then follow arena signage. Allow 10–15 minutes walking time from the Earth Tower.
