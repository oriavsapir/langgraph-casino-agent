# Mohegan Sun — Property Overview

## About Mohegan Sun

**Beyond Your Wildest** — Mohegan Sun is one of the largest and most spectacular casino resorts in the United States. Owned and operated by the Mohegan Tribe of Connecticut, this world-class destination features over 364,000 square feet of gaming space, over 30 world-class retail options, over 35 restaurants and cafés, award-winning dining, an 18-hole championship golf course, a luxurious day spa, and a 10,000-seat arena that hosts top-tier entertainment year-round.

## Getting Here

### By Car
Mohegan Sun is easily accessible from Interstate 395. Take Exit 79A and follow the signs. Self-parking and valet parking are both available on-site.

### Parking
- **Self-parking**: Mohegan Sun offers 13,000+ complimentary parking spaces including:
  - **Riverview Garage** — Closest to Casino of the Sky and Mohegan Sun Arena
  - **Indian Summer Garage** — Direct access to Casino of the Earth
  - **Winter Garage & Winter Lot** — Near the Winter entrance
- **Valet parking**: Available 24/7 at the Sky Tower Hotel and Sky Valet entrances. Fees may apply; complimentary for Momentum members at certain tier levels.
- **Reserved parking**: Pricing starts at $30 per spot in Riverview Reserved (check ParkWhiz for current pricing).

### By Bus
Mohegan Sun welcomes charter buses and offers line-run bus service from various locations. For charter bus information, email charterreservations@mohegansun.com. See the Getting Here page for line-run bus schedules.

**Note**: There will be no shuttle or ferry service starting October 13th, 2025. Check back in May 2026 for the 2026 schedule.

## The Two Casinos

Mohegan Sun features two distinct casino environments:

### Casino of the Earth
The original casino, featuring a warm and earthy design with natural stone, wood, and autumn colors. This casino is home to the Shops at Mohegan Sun, the Mohegan Sun Golf Club, and numerous dining options.

### Casino of the Sky
A more modern and airy casino with a celestial theme, soaring ceilings, and a dramatic planetarium-style dome. This section houses the Sky Tower hotel, the Mohegan Sun Arena, and additional dining and entertainment venues.

The casino floor is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Individual restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues maintain their own hours.

## Age Policy

Guests must be 21 years of age or older to enter the gaming areas. Guests under 21 may visit non-gaming areas such as restaurants, shops, the hotel, and the arena with a parent or guardian.
