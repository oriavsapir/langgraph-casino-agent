# Mohegan Sun — Hotel & Accommodations

Mohegan Sun offers luxurious accommodations across two distinctive hotel towers, with a combined total of over 1,600 rooms and suites.

## Sky Tower

### Overview
The Sky Tower is a AAA Four Diamond Award-winning, 34-story luxury hotel connected directly to Casino of the Sky. It is the resort's premier accommodation, offering sweeping views of the Connecticut countryside and the Thames River.

### Room Types

#### Deluxe King Room
- **Size**: 450 sq ft
- **Bed**: One king bed
- **Max Occupancy**: 2 guests
- **Features**: Floor-to-ceiling windows, marble bathroom with soaking tub and separate rain shower, Frette Italian linens, 55" flat-screen TV, Keurig coffee maker, in-room safe, mini-bar, work desk, high-speed Wi-Fi (complimentary)
- **Rate**: Starting from $249/night (varies by season and availability)

#### Deluxe Double Room
- **Size**: 450 sq ft
- **Beds**: Two queen beds
- **Max Occupancy**: 4 guests
- **Features**: Same amenities as Deluxe King
- **Rate**: Starting from $259/night

#### Sky Tower Suite
- **Size**: 900 sq ft
- **Bed**: One king bed with separate living area
- **Max Occupancy**: 4 guests
- **Features**: Spacious living room with sofa, dining table, oversized marble bathroom with whirlpool tub, walk-in closet, two 55" flat-screen TVs, premium mini-bar, Bose sound system, bathrobes and slippers, turndown service
- **Rate**: Starting from $449/night

#### Presidential Suite
- **Size**: 1,500 sq ft
- **Bed**: One king bed with expansive living and dining areas
- **Max Occupancy**: 4 guests
- **Features**: Full living room, formal dining area, pantry kitchen, two full bathrooms, panoramic views, private check-in, dedicated concierge service, complimentary valet parking
- **Rate**: Available upon request

## Earth Tower

### Overview
The Earth Tower is a 12-story hotel connected to Casino of the Earth, offering a more intimate and value-oriented accommodation experience. Rooms feature the same high-quality appointments in a slightly more compact format.

### Room Types

#### Standard King Room
- **Size**: 375 sq ft
- **Bed**: One king bed
- **Max Occupancy**: 2 guests
- **Features**: Flat-screen TV, granite bathroom, Keurig coffee maker, in-room safe, work desk, high-speed Wi-Fi (complimentary)
- **Rate**: Starting from $179/night

#### Standard Double Room
- **Size**: 375 sq ft
- **Beds**: Two double beds
- **Max Occupancy**: 4 guests
- **Features**: Same amenities as Standard King
- **Rate**: Starting from $189/night

## Check-In & Check-Out

- **Check-In Time**: 4:00 PM
- **Check-Out Time**: 11:00 AM
- **Early Check-In**: Subject to availability; may be requested at the front desk. Momentum members at certain tiers may receive guaranteed early check-in.
- **Late Check-Out**: Subject to availability; may be requested at the front desk. A fee may apply for very late departures.
- **Express Check-Out**: Available via in-room TV or by dropping the key card at the front desk.

## Hotel Amenities

- **Wi-Fi**: Complimentary high-speed internet in all rooms and public areas
- **Fitness Center**: 24-hour fitness center in the Sky Tower with cardio equipment, free weights, and Peloton bikes. Access for hotel guests only.
- **Indoor Pool**: Heated indoor pool and hot tub located on the 2nd floor of the Sky Tower. Open daily 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Towels provided. Hotel guests only.
- **Business Center**: Located in the Sky Tower lobby, open 24 hours with printing, faxing, and computer access.
- **Concierge**: Available in the Sky Tower lobby daily from 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM for restaurant reservations, transportation, event tickets, and local recommendations.
- **Room Service**: Available 24 hours in the Sky Tower; 6:00 AM – 12:00 AM in the Earth Tower.
- **Laundry & Dry Cleaning**: Same-day service available when items are submitted by 9:00 AM.
- **Cribs & Rollaway Beds**: Available upon request at no charge (subject to availability).

## Pet Policy

Mohegan Sun does not allow pets in the hotel, with the exception of certified service animals. Service animals must be identified at check-in. There are no on-site pet-sitting or kennel facilities.

## Smoking Policy

All hotel rooms are non-smoking. Designated smoking areas are available on the casino floor. A cleaning fee of $250 will be charged for evidence of smoking in non-smoking rooms.

## ADA Accessibility

ADA-accessible rooms are available in both the Sky Tower and Earth Tower. These rooms feature wider doorways, roll-in showers, lowered fixtures, and visual/auditory alert systems. Please request an accessible room at the time of booking.
