# Mohegan Sun — Property Overview

## About Mohegan Sun

Mohegan Sun is one of the largest and most spectacular casino resorts in the United States. Owned and operated by the Mohegan Tribe of Connecticut, this world-class destination features over 364,000 square feet of gaming space, hundreds of shops, award-winning dining, championship golf, a luxurious day spa, and a state-of-the-art arena that hosts top-tier entertainment year-round.

## Getting Here

### By Car
Mohegan Sun is easily accessible from Interstate 395. Take Exit 79A and follow the signs. Self-parking and valet parking are both available on-site.

### Parking
- **Self-parking**: Complimentary for all guests in the multi-level parking garages attached to both Casino of the Earth and Casino of the Sky.
- **Valet parking**: Available at the main entrances of both casinos. Fees may apply; complimentary for Momentum members at certain tier levels.

### By Bus
Mohegan Sun offers a motorcoach program with daily bus service from various locations across the northeastern United States. Guests arriving by bus receive bonus packages that may include free-play credits and dining vouchers.

## The Two Casinos

Mohegan Sun features two distinct casino environments:

### Casino of the Earth
The original casino, featuring a warm and earthy design with natural stone, wood, and autumn colors. This casino is home to the Shops at Mohegan Sun, the Mohegan Sun Country Club, and numerous dining options.

### Casino of the Sky
A more modern and airy casino with a celestial theme, soaring ceilings, and a dramatic planetarium-style dome. This section houses the Sky Tower hotel, the Mohegan Sun Arena, and additional dining and entertainment venues.

The casino floor is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Individual restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues maintain their own hours.

## Age Policy

Guests must be 21 years of age or older to enter the gaming areas. Guests under 21 may visit non-gaming areas such as restaurants, shops, the hotel, and the arena with a parent or guardian.
