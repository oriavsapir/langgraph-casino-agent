# Mohegan Sun — Amenities & Experiences

## Spas at Mohegan Sun / Mandara Spa

### Overview
The Mandara Spa (part of "Spas at Mohegan Sun") is a luxurious full-service spa offering an extensive menu of treatments inspired by healing traditions from around the world. Located on the second floor of the Sky Tower, the spa provides a serene escape from the excitement of the casino floor. "Pamper yourself in luxury."

### Hours
- Monday–Thursday: 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM
- Friday–Sunday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM

### Services
- **Massage**: Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone, aromatherapy, couples massage, sports massage (50 min from $160, 80 min from $230)
- **Facials**: Customized facials, anti-aging treatments, hydrating facials, men's facials (50 min from $150)
- **Body Treatments**: Body wraps, salt scrubs, detox treatments, mud therapy (50 min from $140)
- **Nail Services**: Manicures ($40+), pedicures ($60+), gel services, nail art
- **Hair Salon**: Cuts, styling, blowouts, color, and special-occasion styling
- **Waxing & Grooming**: Full menu of waxing services and men's grooming

### Amenities
Spa guests enjoy access to the relaxation lounge, steam room, sauna, and whirlpool. Complimentary herbal tea, infused water, and light refreshments are provided. Spa guests also receive complimentary access to the fitness center and pool.

### Reservations
Advance booking is recommended, especially for weekends and holidays. Cancellations must be made at least 4 hours prior to the appointment to avoid a cancellation fee.

### Momentum Discounts
Momentum members receive spa service discounts with Momentum Dollars: 5–10% off Sunday–Thursday (Ignite/Leap), 10% off any day (Ascend/Soar).

## Mohegan Sun Golf Club

### Overview
Mohegan Sun Golf Club is an 18-hole championship course located just minutes from the resort. The course was designed by Graham Cooke and features challenging play through the beautiful Connecticut landscape with views of the Thames River.

### Details
- **Holes**: 18
- **Par**: 72
- **Yardage**: 6,879 yards from the back tees
- **Slope/Rating**: 134 / 73.5
- **Season**: April through November (weather permitting)
- **Green Fees**: Starting from $79 on weekdays, $99 on weekends (cart included)
- **Tee Times**: Available by phone or online; advance booking recommended

### Amenities
- Pro shop (Golf Shop) with equipment and apparel — Momentum members get 10% discount
- Greenside Grille & Pub — 10% discount for Momentum members
- Practice facilities including driving range and putting green
- PGA professional instruction available (individual and group lessons)
- On-course beverage cart

## Kids Quest & Cyber Quest

### Kids Quest
- **Location**: Casino of the Earth, lower level
- **Hours**: Sunday–Thursday 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM, Friday–Saturday 10:00 AM – 12:00 AM
- **Ages**: 6 weeks to 12 years
- **Description**: A supervised children's activity center where parents can drop off their children while enjoying the resort. Activities include arts and crafts, building projects, video games, movies, karaoke, and a play structure. Trained, background-checked staff provide a safe and fun environment.
- **Rates**: Starting from $15/hour for the first child, $12/hour for siblings. Multi-hour packages available.
- **Requirements**: Photo ID required for pick-up; emergency contact information must be provided at drop-off.

### Cyber Quest
- **Location**: Adjacent to Kids Quest
- **Hours**: Same as Kids Quest
- **Description**: An arcade featuring the latest video games, racing simulators, prize redemption games, and virtual reality experiences. Open to all ages; children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
- **Cost**: Game-by-game token system; tokens can be purchased at the entrance.

## Fitness Center

- **Location**: Sky Tower, 2nd floor
- **Hours**: Open 24 hours (hotel guests only)
- **Equipment**: Treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, Peloton bikes, free weight area, cable machines, yoga mats and stability balls
- **Amenities**: Complimentary towels, bottled water, and personal headphones available at the front desk. Locker rooms with showers available.

## Indoor Pool & Hot Tub

- **Location**: Sky Tower, 2nd floor
- **Hours**: Daily 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Access**: Hotel guests only; room key required for entry
- **Features**: Heated indoor pool, hot tub/whirlpool, poolside lounge chairs, towel service
- **Rules**: Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult. No outside food or beverages. Proper swim attire required.

## Supercharged Indoor Karting & Trampoline Park

Family fun with indoor go-karting and trampoline park. Part of Mohegan Sun's Family Fun offerings alongside Kids Quest, Cyber Quest, Game On, and Connecticut Sun games.
